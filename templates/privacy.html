Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

FinanceFlow ("we", "us", "our") operates the FinanceFlow platform. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account data: Email address, name, and hashed password when you register.
  • YouTube data: Channel name, subscriber count, video count, and view count obtained via the YouTube Data API v3 with your explicit OAuth consent.
  • Payment data: Plan selection and payment reference numbers. Card data is handled by Stripe and never stored on our servers.
  • Usage data: Videos generated, queue activity, and feature usage to improve the service.

2. How We Use Google / YouTube API Data

FinanceFlow uses the YouTube Data API v3 to:

  • Connect your YouTube channel via OAuth 2.0 (you must explicitly grant permission)
  • Upload videos to your channel on your behalf
  • Read your channel statistics (subscribers, views, video count) to display in your dashboard

We comply with the YouTube API Services Terms of Service and the Google Privacy Policy. You can revoke FinanceFlow's access to your Google account at any time at myaccount.google.com/permissions.

3. Third-Party Services

  • OpenAI: Script generation. Prompts are sent to OpenAI's API and subject to OpenAI's privacy policy.
  • ElevenLabs: AI voice generation. Audio files are sent to ElevenLabs and subject to their privacy policy.
  • Brevo (Sendinblue): Transactional emails (welcome, password reset, plan confirmation).
  • Stripe: Payment processing. We do not store card numbers.

4. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data by emailing support. Generated videos stored locally are removed after 30 days.

5. Security

Passwords are hashed using bcrypt. API keys are stored as environment variables, never in code. YouTube OAuth tokens are stored encrypted in our database.

6. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data at any time. Contact us at privacy@financeflow.app.

7. Cookies

We use a single session cookie to keep you logged in. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

8. Changes

We may update this policy. Significant changes will be communicated via email to registered users.
