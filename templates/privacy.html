FinanceFlow ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data when you use the FinanceFlow platform and services ("Service").

The short version: We collect only what we need to run the service. We don't sell your data. Your YouTube channel data is accessed only with your explicit OAuth consent and used solely to upload and manage videos on your behalf.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Registration data: Your full name, email address, and password (stored as a bcrypt hash — we never store your raw password).
  • Profile data: Optional avatar image, logo, and brand color preferences you upload.
  • Plan data: Your current subscription plan, trial status, and billing history.

YouTube / Google Data

  • OAuth tokens: Access and refresh tokens obtained when you connect your YouTube channel. These are stored securely and used solely to upload videos and read channel statistics on your behalf.
  • Channel statistics: Channel name, YouTube channel ID, subscriber count, view count, video count, and profile picture URL — fetched from the YouTube Data API v3 with your consent.
  • Uploaded video metadata: Titles, descriptions, and video IDs of content uploaded through FinanceFlow.

Payment Information

  • Payment plan selection, amount, and status.
  • For manual payments (bank transfer, JazzCash, EasyPaisa): transaction reference numbers you provide.
  • For Stripe payments: Stripe handles all card processing. We receive only confirmation data (subscription ID, status). We never store card numbers or CVVs.

Usage & Technical Data

  • Videos generated, queue activity, scripts created, and feature interactions — used to improve the service and diagnose issues.
  • IP address and browser user-agent for security purposes (rate limiting, fraud prevention).
  • Error logs to identify and fix bugs.

User-Generated Content

  • Custom video scripts and prompts you write in the dashboard.
  • Video files you upload through the "Upload Your Video" feature.
  • Voice recordings uploaded for voice cloning (via ElevenLabs).

2. How We Use Google / YouTube API Data

FinanceFlow uses the YouTube Data API v3 to provide its core service. Specifically, we use it to:

  • Authenticate your Google account and connect your YouTube channel via OAuth 2.0
  • Upload AI-generated videos to your YouTube channel on your behalf (only when you explicitly request it)
  • Upload video thumbnails to improve click-through rates
  • Read your channel statistics (subscribers, views, video count) to display in your dashboard
  • Refresh your access token automatically to maintain the connection without requiring you to re-authenticate constantly

Important: We comply with the YouTube API Services Terms of Service and the Google Privacy Policy.

You can revoke FinanceFlow's access to your Google account at any time by visiting myaccount.google.com/permissions and removing FinanceFlow. Revoking access will prevent FinanceFlow from uploading future videos to your channel.

We do not access any Google data beyond what is required for the features described above. We do not access your Gmail, Google Drive, Google Docs, or any other Google services.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Service delivery: To generate videos, upload to YouTube, send email notifications, and process payments.
  • Account management: To create and maintain your account, send password resets, and provide plan access.
  • Service improvement: Aggregate, anonymized usage data helps us improve features and fix bugs.
  • Communications: Welcome emails, trial reminders, and plan confirmation emails. We do not send unsolicited marketing without your consent.
  • Security: To detect fraud, prevent abuse, and protect the integrity of our platform.
  • Legal compliance: To meet legal obligations where required.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate FinanceFlow. Each has its own privacy policy:

  • OpenAI: Video script generation. Your prompts and niche information are sent to OpenAI's API. Subject to OpenAI's Privacy Policy. We do not send personally identifiable information to OpenAI.
  • ElevenLabs: AI voice synthesis. Your video scripts are sent to ElevenLabs to generate voice audio. If you use voice cloning, your uploaded audio sample is also sent. Subject to ElevenLabs' Privacy Policy.
  • Brevo (Sendinblue): Transactional email service for welcome emails, password resets, and plan notifications. Subject to Brevo's Privacy Policy.
  • Stripe: Payment processing for card payments. Card data is sent directly to Stripe and never passes through our servers. Subject to Stripe's Privacy Policy.
  • YouTube / Google: As described in Section 2. Subject to the Google Privacy Policy.
  • Railway / Render: Cloud hosting infrastructure. Your data is stored on servers managed by these providers. Subject to their respective privacy policies.

5. Data Storage & Security

  • Passwords: Hashed using bcrypt with a strong work factor. We never store or log plaintext passwords.
  • API keys and OAuth tokens: Stored in environment variables on the server or encrypted in the database. Never committed to code repositories.
  • Database: PostgreSQL in production (Railway) with connection encryption. SQLite for local development only.
  • HTTPS: All data in transit is encrypted using TLS.
  • Session cookies: HttpOnly, SameSite=Lax, and Secure (in production).
  • Generated video files: Stored on the server for processing and upload. Temporary files are cleaned up after upload to YouTube.

While we implement industry-standard security measures, no system is 100% secure. We encourage you to use a strong, unique password and enable two-factor authentication on your Google account.

6. Data Retention

  • Account data: Retained for as long as your account is active. If you request deletion, we will remove your data within 30 days.
  • Generated video files: Temporary files used during rendering are deleted after upload. We do not permanently store copies of your generated videos on our servers.
  • User-uploaded video files: Files you upload via the "Upload Your Video" feature are stored until the upload to YouTube is complete, then removed.
  • Payment records: Retained for 7 years to comply with financial record-keeping requirements.
  • Error logs: Retained for up to 90 days for debugging purposes.

7. Cookies & Tracking

  • Session cookie: A single session cookie is used to keep you logged in across browser sessions. This is strictly necessary for the service to function.
  • No advertising cookies: We do not use advertising cookies, retargeting pixels, or third-party tracking scripts (e.g., Meta Pixel, Google Analytics).
  • No fingerprinting: We do not use browser fingerprinting techniques.

8. Your Rights & Choices

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data. You can update your name and password directly from the dashboard settings.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and all associated data. Contact us at privacy@financeflow.app.
  • Portability: Request your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Objection: Object to certain processing of your data.
  • Revoke YouTube access: Visit myaccount.google.com/permissions to revoke FinanceFlow's access to your Google account at any time.

To exercise these rights, email privacy@financeflow.app. We will respond within 30 days.

9. Children's Privacy

FinanceFlow is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you believe a minor has registered, please contact us immediately and we will delete the account.
