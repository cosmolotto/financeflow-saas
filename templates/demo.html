Frequently Asked Questions
Does FinanceFlow post to my YouTube without my permission?
No. Videos are only uploaded when you click Generate (or when Autopilot is enabled — which you opt in to explicitly). You can disable Autopilot or revoke YouTube access at any time.
What YouTube permissions does FinanceFlow request?
We request youtube.upload (to upload videos) and youtube.readonly (to read channel stats). We do not access Gmail, Drive, or any other Google service.
How do I revoke access?
Visit myaccount.google.com/permissions, find FinanceFlow, and click Remove. You can also disconnect from inside the FinanceFlow dashboard.
Are the generated videos original content?
Yes. Scripts are generated fresh each time by GPT-4, voiceovers are synthesised by ElevenLabs, and frames are rendered programmatically. Each video is unique.
What AI is used for the voice?
ElevenLabs multilingual voice synthesis (model eleven_multilingual_v2). You can also upload your own voice sample to use your voice.
